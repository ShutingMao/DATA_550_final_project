Exploring the state of transitional care in pediatric pilocytic astrocytoma data analysis

Shuting Mao

2024-12-01

Introduction

Pediatric pilocytic astrocytoma (PPA) requires prolonged follow-up after initial resection. The landscape of transitional care for PPA patients is not well characterized. The authors sought to examine the clinical course and transition to adult care for these patients to better characterize opportunities for improvement in long-term care. The analysis will focus on creating descriptive summaries, performing group comparisons (e.g., pediatric vs. adult patients), and presenting these findings in a series of tables and figures to better illustrate the clinical landscape and care transitions for PPA patients. Key insights will be derived from both statistical tests and visualizations (bar charts, pie charts, etc.) to provide a comprehensive overview of the long-term care needs of this patient population.
